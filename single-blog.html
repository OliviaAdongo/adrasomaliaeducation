about us

WCGA

At WGCA, we are a leading strategic communications firm with a deep commitment to impactful storytelling across Africa. With extensive experience in multimedia production, we specialize in capturing and conveying stories that resonate, inspire, and drive change. Our expertise lies in transforming complex ideas into compelling narratives that communicate the transformative impact of programs and initiatives.
We take pride in our ability to create high-impact multimedia content that not only showcases success stories but also informs future program development and strengthens advocacy efforts. Our work spans across various sectors, and we are particularly passionate about supporting projects that have a lasting, positive impact on communities.

Currently, we are proud to partner with ADRA in Somalia, where we are documenting best practices, lessons learned, and success stories from their transformative projects. Through our strategic approach and dedicated methodology, we ensure that every story we tell is backed by a solid process that emphasizes relevance, clarity, and impact.

With a clear focus on delivering structured and meaningful documentation, WGCA is committed to providing the insights and resources necessary for advocacy and future program development. Our mission is to amplify the voices that matter and ensure that every story we tell contributes to a better and more informed future.

WGCA is committed to delivering a world-class documentation project that showcases the transformative impact of ADRA’s interventions in Somalia. Through a research-driven, high-quality multimedia approach, WGCA will ensure that the project’s stories reach the right audiences and drive meaningful engagement.

Expected Outcomes

i. Enhanced Visibility –

High-quality documentation that strengthens ADRA’s advocacy and donor engagement.

ii. Knowledge Sharing –

A well-structured final report that captures lessons learned and best practices.

iii. Sustainable Impact –

Content that informs future programs and helps ADRA communicate its successes effectively.
