our objectives

Impact

i. Document and showcase the impact of ADRA’s interventions through high-quality multimedia content.

Best Practices

ii. Capture best practices and lessons learned to inform future program design and implementation.

Advocacy

iii. Develop advocacy and awareness materials for stakeholders, donors, and the broader public.

Report

iv. Provide ADRA with organized, well-structured documentation, including raw footage, processed materials, and a final report summarizing key findings.

our scope

Video Production
  1. Five (5) short documentary videos (1.5 minutes each) capturing success stories from beneficiaries and stakeholders.
  2. Four (4) interview videos featuring state officials, donors, and project beneficiaries.
Photography & Publications
  1. 100+ high-quality images documenting project achievements.
  2. Three (3) photobooks compiling key moments and highlights
Digital & Social Media Content
  1. Social media materials, including reels, infographics, and a structured content calendar.
Additional Deliverables
  1. Two (2) hard drives containing all raw footage and images.
  2. Three (3) professionally printed photobooks (A3 size).
  3. A final report summarizing findings, best practices, and recommendations.

Methodology

Research & Planning

  • Inception Meeting: An initial meeting with ADRA to align on project goals, key messages, and expectations.
  • Desk Review: Analysis of project reports, previous documentation, and other relevant materials.
  • Storyboarding & Interview Planning: Identifying key stories, selecting interviewees, and preparing questions

Production

  • On-Site Fieldwork: Video interviews, photography, and documentation of project activities.
  • Stakeholder Engagement: Interviews with government officials, donors, and community members.

Post-Production & Delivery

  • Editing & Content Development: High-quality video and photo editing, creation of social media assets, and microsite development.
  • Review & Feedback: Two rounds of reviews with ADRA to refine content.
  • Final Submission: Delivery of all materials, including raw footage and a final report with lessons learned.
